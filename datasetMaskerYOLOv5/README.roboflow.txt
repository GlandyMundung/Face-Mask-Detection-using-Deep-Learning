
deteksi_masker - v2 facemask_dataset
==============================

This dataset was exported via roboflow.ai on November 25, 2021 at 3:54 PM GMT

It includes 400 images.
Mask-and-no-mask are annotated in YOLO v5 PyTorch format.

The following pre-processing was applied to each image:
* Resize to 640x640 (Stretch)

No image augmentation techniques were applied.
